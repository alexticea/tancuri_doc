# Privacy Policy for Tanks 2026

**Effective Date:** April 2026

At Tanks 2026, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our dApp.

## 1. Data We Collect
- **Public Wallet Address:** When you connect your Solana wallet, we record your public address to track your scores, rank, and rewards.
- **Game Performance:** We store your scores, levels reached, and gameplay statistics on our servers to maintain the Global Leaderboard.
- **Social IDs:** If you use the Telegram reward system, we store your association with our Telegram bot to verify task completion.

## 2. What We DO NOT Collect
- **Private Keys:** We never have access to your private keys or seed phrases. All transactions are signed by you through your own wallet provider (non-custodial).
- **Personal Identity:** We do not collect names, emails, or phone numbers unless provided voluntarily through external social channels.

## 3. How Data is Used
- To manage the Global Leaderboard.
- To verify and distribute $SKR rewards.
- To prevent cheating and ensure fair play.

## 4. Blockchain Transparency
Please note that all token transfers and SKR distributions occur on the Solana blockchain and are public by nature.

## 5. Third Parties
We use infrastructure like Solana Mainnet and MongoDB/Node.js servers to power the game. We do not sell your data to third parties.

## Contact
If you have questions about this policy, please open an issue in the official project repository.
